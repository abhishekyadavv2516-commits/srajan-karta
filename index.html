Meet the Designer

Isha Verma

Interior Designer & Nature Artist

With a deep passion for merging functional design with the organic beauty of nature, Isha Verma founded Srajan Karta to create spaces that truly live and breathe. Her philosophy is simple — every space tells a story, and every story deserves to be told beautifully.

Armed with expertise in AutoCAD and SketchUp for precision layouts and an artist's eye for living decor, Isha brings a rare blend of technical skill and creative vision to every project. From luxury residential interiors to intricate miniature landscapes, her work reflects a commitment to harmony between human spaces and the natural world.

Based in Bhopal, she works with clients who value spaces that are not just designed, but thoughtfully crafted — places where you can feel the intention in every corner, every plant, and every ray of light.
